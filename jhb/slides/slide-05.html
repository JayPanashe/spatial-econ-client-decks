Tier 4 · Johannesburg operational layer · BRING YOUR OWN

Your data fills this tier.

Tiers 1–3 cover Johannesburg from the national backbone today. Tier 4 is the metro-or-province-private operational layer that turns the platform from a research surface into an operating system. For Johannesburg this would be your municipal billing, fault, valuation roll, incentive-rebate, fDi, firm-registry, council, and informal-settlement feeds.
